Understanding Vehicle Finance

Getting financing for your new vehicle doesn't have to be complicated. Our finance calculator helps you understand your monthly obligations and plan your budget effectively.

  • Vehicle Price: The total purchase price of the vehicle you're interested in.
  • Deposit Amount: The upfront payment you make to reduce the loan amount.
  • Interest Rate: The annual percentage rate charged by the lender.
  • Loan Term: The duration over which you'll repay the loan (typically 12-84 months).

Remember that your monthly payment includes both principal (the amount borrowed) and interest (the cost of borrowing). Making a larger deposit or choosing a shorter loan term will reduce the total interest paid over the life of the loan.
